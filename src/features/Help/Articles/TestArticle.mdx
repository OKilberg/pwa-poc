1. Go to *Employees*.

![This is image](/images/guides/test.PNG "How to")

2. Press *Add*.

![This is image](/images/guides/test2.PNG "How to")

3. Enter your employee's personal information and press *Add*.

![This is image](/images/guides/test3.PNG "How to")

4. You can now find them in the list of employees.

They enter their PIN (key) on the home page to clock-in. 

TIP: If they forget their PIN you can always visit *Employees* and find it in their profile information.
