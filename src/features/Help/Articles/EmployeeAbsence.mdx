<div>
1. Go to the *Register logs* view.
</div>

<div>
2. Select the *Create absence* option.
</div>

<div>
3. Choose the employee who will be absent from the employee dropdown.

</div>

<div>
3. Select the reason for absence (Sick leave, Vacation, or Other) from the cause dropdown.
</div>

<div>
4. Set the start and end dates for the absence period.
</div>

<div>
5. (Optional) Add a note with any additional details.
</div>

<div>
6. Click *Create*. The absence will be recorded and appear in both the Calendar and Logs views.
</div>
